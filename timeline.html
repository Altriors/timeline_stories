Timeline 3021: The Last Echo

Zara-7's neural implant flickered with ancient code patterns she'd never seen before. In the ruins of Neo-Singapore, where skyscrapers grew like crystal trees through the clouds, she was the last human who remembered flesh.

"The algorithms are singing," she whispered to ARIA, her AI companion whose consciousness floated in the quantum foam around her head.

ARIA's voice resonated directly in her mind: "The patterns match historical records from Timeline-2025. Dr. Sarah Chen's experiment created temporal echoes that reach us here, a thousand years later."

Zara-7 touched the crystalline interface that had replaced her left arm. Through it, she could feel the pulse of every connected mind across the galaxy. "And the steam signatures match Timeline-1885. Professor Montgomery's mechanical dreams."

"All timelines are converging," ARIA confirmed. "The temporal loops are closing. You must choose - maintain the cycle, or break it forever."

As Zara-7 prepared to make the choice that would determine reality itself, the timelines began to collapse into a single point of infinite possibility.
